Guest Policies & Etiquette

To ensure a peaceful and premium environment for all our guests, we adhere to strict house policies.

Check-In / Out

Standard timing is 12:00 PM for both check-in and check-out. We operate 24 hours to accommodate special travel schedules.

Strictly Enforced

Family & Couples

We are a family-friendly establishment. Unmarried couples are strictly prohibited. Valid CNIC or proof of marriage is required at check-in.

Payments

Full payment is required upon check-in to confirm your stay. We accept cash and major bank transfers on site.
